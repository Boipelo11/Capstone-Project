About us

A safe space for young people to share their voices, dreams and networks. We enjoy watching them break into multi-million industries, making positive changes in their communities and nourishing the roots of our beautiful African continent. Other exciting features are local events which happen on National Holidays such as June 16, we follow what the youth of Mzansi gets up to and share the experiences on our platform.

Don't forget to check out the job opportunities to get career guidance and see which companies are currently seeking youth employees. Your future employer or business partners could be just a click away!
